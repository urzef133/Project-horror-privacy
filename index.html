Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Who we are

“Project Horror” is a VR multiplayer game available on the Meta Horizon Store. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. What data we collect

When you play “Project Horror”, the following data may be collected:

  • Your Meta account display name (public)
  • Technical data such as device type, VR headset model, and app version
  • Gameplay-related activity (e.g., session duration, game progress)

3. How we use your data

We only use this data to improve gameplay experience, analyze bugs, and maintain technical performance. We do not use your data for advertising purposes or share it with third parties.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely and only accessible by authorized personnel. We use appropriate security measures to protect your information.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at:

support@projecthorror-vr.com

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. All changes will be published here.
